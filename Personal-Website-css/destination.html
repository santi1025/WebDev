Winterfell Winterfell is the ancestral seat of House Stark. It is considered the capital of the North. It is located in the center of the northern province of the Seven Kingdoms, near the Royal Road that leads to King's Landing.
King's Landing King's Landing is the capital and largest city of the Six Kingdoms. Located on the east coast of Westeros in the Crownlands, just north of where Blackwater Rush empties into Blackwater Bay and overlooking Blackwater Bay, King's Landing is the site of the Iron Throne and the Red Keep, the seat of the King of the Andals and the First Men
Storm's end Storm's End is the seat of House Baratheon, the Overlords of the Stormlands. The castle is located at Durran's Point on the north shore of Shipbreaker Bay, [1] south of Bronzegate and northeast of Griffin's Roost. The Kingsroad runs north from Storm's End through the Stormlands to King's Landing, the capital of the Seven Kingdoms.
Dragonstone Dragonstone, or Dragonstone Castle,[1][N 1] is the castle that stands upon the eponymous island located in Blackwater Bay. It is the ancestral seat of House Targaryen and the former stronghold of House Baratheon of Dragonstone, a cadet branch of House Baratheon of Storm's End. It is within the Crownlands, the capital region of the Seven Kingdoms.
